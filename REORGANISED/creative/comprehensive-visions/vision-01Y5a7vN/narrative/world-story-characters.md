## PART 1: WORLD-BUILDING

### Core Concept: Harmonia
**The World Where Music Is Life**

In Harmonia, music isn't art—it's the fundamental force of reality. The world literally runs on rhythm. When the Great Silence began corrupting the world's music, everything started falling apart.

---

### The Setting (10 Variations)

**Option 1: Post-Apocalyptic Harmony**
After a cataclysm silenced 90% of the world's music, survivors struggle to keep rhythm alive. Players restore musical zones one by one.

**Option 2: Musical Dimensions**
Multiple worlds each representing a music genre. Travel between dimensions to master all musical forms.

**Option 3: Living Instrument World**
The planet itself is a massive instrument. Different biomes are different instrument sections. Mountains = percussion, oceans = winds, forests = strings.

**Option 4: Time-Traveling Musician**
Journey through musical history from ancient drums to electronic music, learning from each era.

**Option 5: Dream Realm of Music**
Physical world is silent. Music only exists in collective unconscious. Players dive into dreams to restore music to reality.

**Option 6: Rebel Against Silence**
Authoritarian regime banned music. Underground resistance fights with rhythm. Revolution through song.

**Option 7: Music School Chronicles**
Magical conservatory where students learn to wield music as power. Coming-of-age story.

**Option 8: Wandering Troubadour**
Travel from town to town, solving problems with music. Episodic structure.

**Option 9: Cosmic Symphony**
Universe is a song. Cosmic forces disrupting the melody. Players must restore harmony to existence itself.

**Option 10: Musical Ecosystem**
Natural world operates on rhythm. Hunt monsters not with violence, but by out-performing them musically.

**RECOMMENDED: Hybrid of 1, 3, and 6**
- World powered by music (3)
- Corruption threatening it (1)
- Factions with different musical philosophies (6)

### Lore & Mythology (25 Concepts)

**Creation Myth:**
In the beginning, there was only Silence. Then the First Note rang out, and from it, all reality vibrated into being. The First Song created the elements, the land, the sky, and finally, life itself.

**The Three Composers:**
Ancient beings who shaped reality:
1. **Melodia** - Created beauty, harmony, emotions
2. **Rhythmos** - Created structure, time, patterns
3. **Dissonax** - Created chaos, change, evolution

**The Great Silence (Antagonist Force):**
Entropy given consciousness. It seeks to return everything to primordial Silence. Not evil, just oppositional to existence.

**Musical Elements:**
- Fire = Percussion (drum of heartbeat)
- Water = Strings (flowing melodies)
- Air = Winds (breath of life)
- Earth = Bass (foundation rhythm)
- Lightning = Brass (bright, sharp)
- Ice = Crystals (pure tones)
- Nature = Woodwinds (organic sounds)
- Void = Electronic (artificial, new)

**Ancient Civilizations:**
1. **Drum Empire** - First civilization, percussion-based
2. **String Dynasty** - Classical golden age
3. **Wind Kingdoms** - Nomadic tribes
4. **Electronic Collective** - Modern rising power

**Legendary Artifacts:**
1. Baton of the First Conductor
2. Original Drum (creation myth)
3. Strings of Melodia
4. Perfect Pitch Stone
5. Metronome of Eternal Time
6. Symphony Score of Reality
7. Silencer's Crown (antagonist artifact)
(+15 more legendary items)

**Musical Creatures:**
Monsters aren't mindless—they're living music that has lost harmony with the world.

**The Rhythm of Seasons:**
World operates on musical cycles:
- Spring = Allegro (fast, energetic)
- Summer = Vivace (lively)
- Autumn = Andante (walking pace)
- Winter = Adagio (slow, calm)

**Musical Languages:**
Different cultures speak in musical patterns. Some communicate entirely through instrument playing.

**The Unheard:**
Tragic souls who lost their ability to perceive music. Living in silence while world vibrates around them.

**Musical Afterlife:**
Death is joining the Eternal Symphony—becoming one note in infinite song.

**Rhythm Guardians:**
Spiritual beings maintaining world's tempo. If they fail, time itself becomes unstable.

**Harmony Wells:**
Natural springs where music is purest. Sites of power and pilgrimage.

**The Cacophony:**
Dark reflection of music—discord, noise, chaos. Dangerous but necessary for balance.

**Musical Memory:**
Past events leave "echo recordings" in locations. Players can experience historical moments.

**Instrument Souls:**
Every instrument has a spirit. Master craftspeople can awaken them.

**The Silent Monks:**
Order that believes ultimate enlightenment is beyond music. Antagonists or misunderstood allies?

**Genre Wars:**
Historical conflicts between musical philosophies. Scars still visible in world.

**The Underground Rhythm:**
Secret pulse beneath world's surface. Some claim it's the planet's heartbeat.

**Musical Weather:**
Storms, rain, wind—all have rhythm patterns. Weather responds to world's musical state.

**The Resonance:**
Mystical connection between all music. Skilled musicians can tap into it.

**Forbidden Frequencies:**
Certain note combinations are banned—they can warp reality dangerously.

**The Last Silence:**
Prophesied end of all music. Players might be preventing it... or causing it.

**Musical Reincarnation:**
Great musicians are reborn, carrying echo of past lives' abilities.

**The First Listener:**
Mythical being who heard the First Song and taught it to mortals.

## Dialogue & Writing Style

**Tone: Warm, Hopeful, Musical**
- Metaphors drawn from music constantly
- Puns acceptable but not overdone
- Emotional but not melodramatic
- Humor through character, not jokes
- Serious themes treated respectfully

**Character Voice Examples:**

**Drumbeat (Mentor):**
"Listen kid, rhythm is like a heartbeat. Miss it, you die. Simple as that."

**Blue Note (Jazz Pianist):**
"Darling, improvisation is like a conversation. You listen, you respond, you riff. Don't plan it. Feel it."

**Maestro Forte (Conductor):**
"Precision! Every note must be perfect. Music is mathematics, not finger-painting!"

**Nexus (DJ):**
"Yo, check it—music is evolution. If you're not creating the future, you're stuck in the past."

**Willow Song (Folk Wanderer):**
"The wind sings if you listen. The trees keep time. Nature is the first musician."

**Diversity Note:**
- Characters from different cultures
- Musical traditions respected
- No stereotypes
- Authentic representation where possible
- Consultants for cultural music

## Unique Narrative Selling Points

1. **Music IS the magic system** - Not metaphor, literal
2. **Deaf protagonist option** - Representation + unique mechanic
3. **Multiple story paths** - Genre choice affects narrative
4. **Living world** - NPCs have lives beyond player
5. **Generational play** - Choices affect future generations (NG+)
6. **Musical languages** - Communication through instruments
7. **Emotional depth** - Music allows deep feeling expression
8. **Educational** - Real music theory woven in
9. **No chosen one** - Earn heroism through skill
10. **Consequences matter** - Choices persist

**Innovation:** First rhythm game with Witcher 3-level narrative depth.
