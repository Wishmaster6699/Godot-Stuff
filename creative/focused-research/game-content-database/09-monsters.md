## ENEMY MECHANICS

### Aggro Types
- **Passive**: Won't attack unless hit
- **Neutral**: Attacks if too close
- **Aggressive**: Chases on sight
- **Boss**: Always aggressive

### Attack Patterns
- **Melee**: Close range physical
- **Ranged**: Projectile attacks
- **Magic**: Elemental spells
- **AOE**: Area damage
- **Support**: Buffs other enemies

### Special Abilities
- **Summon**: Calls reinforcements
- **Heal**: Restores HP
- **Buff**: Enhances stats
- **Debuff**: Weakens player
- **Counter**: Retaliates when hit

### Weaknesses
- **Genre**: Weak to opposite genre
- **Element**: Weak to counter element
- **Rhythm**: Vulnerable during off-beat
- **Instrument**: Weak to specific weapon
- **Status**: Susceptible to certain debuffs

### Resistances
- **Physical**: Armor against weapons
- **Magical**: Magic defense
- **Elemental**: Resistant to elements
- **Status**: Immune to debuffs
- **Genre**: Strong vs certain music

## ENEMY BEHAVIORS

### Swarm Enemies
- Appear in groups
- Weak individually
- Strong together
- Combo attacks

### Solo Enemies
- Appear alone
- Stronger than swarm
- Unique abilities
- Boss-like patterns

### Elite Variants
- Golden/shiny versions
- 5x normal stats
- Better drops
- Rare spawns

### Environmental
- Camouflaged enemies
- Ambush predators
- Territory defenders
- Weather-dependent

### Time-Based
- Day only
- Night only
- Specific times
- Seasonal spawns
