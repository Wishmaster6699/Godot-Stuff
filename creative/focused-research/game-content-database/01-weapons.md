## WEAPON MECHANICS

### Rarity Tiers
- **Common**: Basic instruments (10-20 damage)
- **Uncommon**: Quality instruments (20-40 damage)
- **Rare**: Master-crafted (40-70 damage)
- **Epic**: Legendary musicians' gear (70-120 damage)
- **Legendary**: One-of-a-kind mythical (120-200+ damage)

### Special Weapon Properties
- **Tempo Sync**: Damage increases with combo meter
- **Harmony Bonus**: Extra damage when playing with allies
- **Rhythm Critical**: Perfect timing = critical hits
- **Genre Mastery**: Bonus damage vs. specific enemy types
- **Acoustic/Electric**: Different playstyles and effects
- **Element Alignment**: Fire, Ice, Lightning, Wind, Earth, Light, Dark, Sound

### Upgrade Paths
- Tune instrument for stat boosts
- Add strings/reeds for new abilities
- Enchant with musical runes
- Fuse with other instruments
- Awaken legendary forms
