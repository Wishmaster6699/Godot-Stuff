## Technical Implementation Considerations

### Performance Optimization
- Particle pooling (reuse objects)
- LOD for distant effects
- Disable particles at low FPS
- Scalable visual quality settings

### Accessibility Options
- Reduce motion option (less screen shake/flash)
- High contrast mode (clear shapes over particles)
- Colorblind modes (pattern + color)
- Photosensitivity mode (no flashing)

### Visual Priority System
**High Priority (Always Render):**
- Timing indicators
- Hit/miss confirmation
- Critical gameplay UI

**Medium Priority (Scale with Performance):**
- Particle effects
- Character animations
- Environmental reactions

**Low Priority (Disable if Needed):**
- Background animations
- Ambient particles
- Screen effects
- Post-processing
