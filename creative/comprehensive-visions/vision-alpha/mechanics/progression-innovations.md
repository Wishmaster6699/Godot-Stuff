## 1. Musical Skill Trees (Ideas 1-12)

### 1. **Genre Mastery Paths**
Separate skill trees for music genres:

**Rock Tree:**
- Power Chord Strikes (+25% heavy attack damage)
- Distortion Wave (attacks have AOE effect)
- Headbang Recovery (regain health on perfect timing)
- Stage Dive (aerial slam attack)
- Feedback Loop (damage returns to enemies)

**Jazz Tree:**
- Improvisation (off-beat attacks deal +30% damage)
- Swing Feel (timing windows more forgiving)
- Blue Note Strike (attacks ignore armor)
- Scat Dodge (unique dodge animation + i-frames)
- Cool Solo (time slows during combo)

**Classical Tree:**
- Perfect Precision (timing windows tighter but +50% damage)
- Orchestral Might (summon instrument attacks)
- Conductor's Authority (nearby NPCs fight better)
- Crescendo Build (damage increases over combat duration)
- Finale Burst (massive attack after sustained combat)

**Electronic Tree:**
- Synth Speed (attack speed +25%)
- Beat Drop (devastating periodic attack)
- Modulation Shield (cycling damage resistances)
- Glitch Step (teleport dodge)
- Filter Sweep (projectile attacks curve)

**Hip-Hop Tree:**
- Flow State (combo chains last longer)
- Scratch Technique (quick damage bursts)
- Bass Boost (heavy attacks even heavier)
- Freestyle (random positive effects)
- Breakdance Combat (dodge + damage simultaneously)

**World Music Tree:**
- Polyrhythm Mastery (dual rhythm attacks)
- Cultural Fusion (combine other tree abilities)
- Tribal Power (summon ancestral spirits)
- Meditation Recovery (heal while standing still on beat)
- Global Harmony (party-wide buffs)

**Priority**: v1.5 (Core identity system)
**Implementation**: Extend progression system with branching trees

### 2. **Instrument Proficiency System**
Gain experience with each instrument type:

**Levels 1-10 per instrument:**
- Lv 1: Basic attacks unlocked
- Lv 3: Combo extension +2
- Lv 5: Special move unlocked
- Lv 7: Passive bonus (damage/speed)
- Lv 10: Ultimate technique

**Instrument Categories:**
- Strings (Guitar, Violin, Bass, Harp)
- Percussion (Drums, Xylophone, Cymbals)
- Wind (Flute, Saxophone, Trumpet)
- Keys (Piano, Organ, Synthesizer)
- Electronic (Turntables, Drum Machine, Sampler)
- Exotic (Didgeridoo, Sitar, Steel Drums)

**Cross-Training Bonuses:**
- Master 3 in same family = Family Mastery bonus
- Master 1 of each family = Versatility bonus
- Master all instruments = Maestro Title + special ability

**Priority**: v1.5
**Synergy**: Equipment system (weapons are instruments)

### 3. **Rhythm Technique Unlocks**
Progression unlocks advanced rhythm abilities:

**Beginner Tier (Levels 1-10):**
- Perfect Timing I (wider perfect windows)
- Beat Chain Starter (combos start easier)
- Basic Syncopation (off-beat detection)

**Intermediate Tier (Levels 11-20):**
- Perfect Timing II (even wider windows)
- Polyrhythm Awareness (track dual rhythms)
- Triplet Mastery (3 hits per beat)
- Measure Extension (combos last longer)

**Advanced Tier (Levels 21-30):**
- Perfect Timing III (maximum windows)
- Cross-Rhythm Expert (independent limb timing)
- Time Signature Flexibility (adapt to changes)
- Rubato Control (tempo flexibility)

**Master Tier (Levels 31+):**
- Absolute Tempo (innate perfect timing)
- Musical Savant (all rhythm techniques mastered)
- Improvisation God (create own patterns for bonuses)

**Priority**: v1.5
**Progression**: Natural skill development through gameplay

### 4. **Conductor's Rank System**
Overall mastery ranking:

**Ranks:**
1. Novice (Level 1-5)
2. Apprentice (Level 6-10)
3. Performer (Level 11-15)
4. Virtuoso (Level 16-20)

### 5. **Musical Theory Knowledge Tree**
Meta-progression through understanding music:

**Learn concepts through gameplay:**
- Rhythm (timing, beat, tempo)
- Melody (pitch, scales, intervals)
- Harmony (chords, progressions)
- Dynamics (volume, intensity)
- Form (structure, composition)

**Each concept has sub-skills:**
Example - **Rhythm branch:**
- Simple Time Signatures (2/4, 3/4, 4/4)
- Complex Time Signatures (5/4, 7/8, 11/8)
- Polymeter Understanding
- Rhythm Reading (sight-reading)

**Benefits:**
- Understanding concepts = gameplay bonuses
- Actual education value (players learn real music theory)
- Optional lore entries explain concepts
- Quiz system for XP bonuses

**Priority**: v2.0 (Educational feature)

### 6. **Style Points & Rank System**
Devil May Cry inspired combat ranking:

**Real-time combat ranking:**
- D, C, B, A, S, SS, SSS ranks
- Based on: variety, timing perfection, no damage taken, combo length

**Style Meter:**
- Fills with varied attacks
- Drains with repeated moves
- Drains when hit
- Builds faster with on-beat perfection

**Rewards for high ranks:**
- End-of-battle bonus XP/items
- Unlockable techniques require S ranks
- Leaderboards for each battle
- Titles for consistent high ranks

**Priority**: v1.5

### 11. **Musical Alignment System** (Expand S21)
Current resonance system expansion:

**Three Axes of Alignment:**

**Rhythm vs Flow:**
- Rhythm: Precise, technical, by-the-book
- Flow: Improvised, creative, flexible
- Affects available techniques and playstyle

**Harmony vs Discord:**
- Harmony: Cooperative, supporting, healing
- Discord: Aggressive, chaotic, damage
- Affects social interactions and abilities

**Tradition vs Innovation:**
- Tradition: Classical, proven, structured
- Innovation: Experimental, risky, unique
- Affects which NPCs respect you

**Dynamic Shifting:**
- Actions shift alignment over time
- No locked choices
- Visible on character screen
- NPCs react to current alignment

**Priority**: v1.5 (Enhances existing S21 system)

## Progression Philosophy

Our progression system should be:

1. **Multi-Faceted**: Many paths to power
2. **Reversible**: Respec freely, experiment safely
3. **Rewarding**: Constant unlocks and discoveries
4. **Educational**: Learn real music concepts
5. **Expressive**: Build unique character identity
6. **Fair**: Skill matters, no pay-to-win
7. **Engaging**: Always something to work toward
8. **Optional**: Can ignore systems and still succeed
