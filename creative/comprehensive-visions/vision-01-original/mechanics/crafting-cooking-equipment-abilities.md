### Crafting Quality System
- **Perfect Rhythm (100% on-beat)**: Masterwork quality, +50% bonus
- **Good Rhythm (85-99% on-beat)**: High quality, +25% bonus
- **Adequate Rhythm (70-84% on-beat)**: Standard quality, baseline
- **Poor Rhythm (50-69% on-beat)**: Low quality, -25% penalty
- **Chaotic Rhythm (<50% on-beat)**: Broken item, -50% penalty

### Rhythm Crafting Synergies
- Craft multiple items in harmony: Group bonus
- Craft items of same instrument family: Resonance bonus
- Craft items in sequence matching harmonic progression: Harmonic bonus
- Maintain perfect rhythm throughout: Legendary quality unlock

## CROSS-SYSTEM SYNERGIES

### Crafting → Equipment
- Crafted items gain special properties
- Perfectly crafted items appear rarer in shops
- Crafting specific items unlocks new equipment types

### Cooking → Abilities
- Consuming specific foods unlocks food-based abilities
- Eating before combat grants temporary boosts
- Specific food combinations unlock special abilities

### Equipment → Abilities
- Wearing specific armor sets unlocks set abilities
- Legendary weapons unlock legendary abilities
- Equipment stats directly modify ability damage/effect

### Progression → All Systems
- Higher progression tiers unlock better crafting recipes
- Better equipment available at higher tiers
- More powerful abilities learned at higher tiers
- Cooking becomes more sophisticated with progression
