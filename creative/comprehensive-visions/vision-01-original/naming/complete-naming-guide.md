## NAMING CONVENTIONS

### Consistency Guidelines
- **String Instruments**: Elegant, precise names
- **Percussion**: Powerful, impactful names
- **Wind Instruments**: Swift, flowing names
- **Keyboard**: Technical, structured names
- **Harmony Concepts**: Peaceful, balanced names
- **Dissonance Concepts**: Chaotic, harsh names

### Cultural Inspirations
- Greek musical terms (Aeolian, Dorian, etc.)
- Latin music terminology
- Real instrument names with fantasy twist
- Musical terminology adapted to fantasy
- Sound-based metaphors

### Memorable Quality
- Names should be pronounceable
- Connection to music/sound clear
- Distinct from each other
- Easy to remember in dialogue
- Thematic consistency throughout
