## Visual Direction Recommendations

**Style Target:**
- **Primary:** Sea of Stars (modern pixel with effects)
- **Secondary:** Eastward (rich detail and color)
- **Accents:** Hyper Light Drifter (neon music effects)
- **Combat:** Dead Cells (fluid animation)
- **UI:** Persona 5 (stylish and clear)

**Resolution:**
- 16x16 base grid for tiles
- 32x32 or larger for characters
- High-res backgrounds (Sea of Stars approach)
- Scaling for modern displays

**Animation Targets:**
- Player: 8-12 frames per animation
- Enemies: 4-8 frames (vary by importance)
- Bosses: 12-16 frames
- Effects: 6-8 frames
- 60 FPS gameplay

**Palette Strategy:**
- Base palette: 64 colors
- Genre-specific extensions
- Lighting/time variations
- Musical effect colors
- Accessibility modes
