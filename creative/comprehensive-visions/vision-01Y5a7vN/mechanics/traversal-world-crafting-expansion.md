### Rhythm-Based Movement (1-15)

**1. Beat-Synced Dash**
- Dash on beat = longer distance + invincibility frames
- Off-beat dash = shorter, vulnerable
- Teaches rhythm through movement
- **Priority:** High

**2. Musical Double Jump**
- Second jump only works if first jump was on-beat
- Creates rhythm awareness during platforming
- Combine movement + combat timing
- **Priority:** High

**3. Grind Rail Rhythm Surfing**
- Speed increases when jumping on rails to the beat
- Miss beat = slow down, fall off
- Reference: Jet Set Radio + rhythm
- **Priority:** High - Existing system enhancement

**4. Tempo Sprint**
- Running speed tied to BPM
- Faster music = faster movement
- Creates urgency and flow
- **Priority:** Medium

**5. Polyrhythm Parkour**
- Wall-run on one beat, jump on another
- Advanced movement requires two rhythm awareness
- High skill ceiling
- **Priority:** Medium

**6. Crescendo Climb**
- Climbing speed increases with combo meter
- Higher rhythm combo = faster climbing
- Rewards sustained accuracy
- **Priority:** Medium

**7. Glissando Glide**
- Gliding slides smoothly between platforms
- Landing on beat = bounce higher
- Smooth, musical movement feel
- **Priority:** Medium

**8. Staccato Steps**
- Quick, precise platform jumps on specific subdivisions
- Precision platforming challenge
- Advanced technique unlock
- **Priority:** Low

**9. Legato Long Jump**
- Hold jump button across multiple beats
- Creates sustained flight
- Different feel from quick jumps
- **Priority:** Low

**10. Syncopation Wall Kick**
- Wall kicks on off-beats only
- Forces different rhythm thinking
- Advanced movement tech
- **Priority:** Low

**11. Arpeggio Ascent**
- Multi-jump sequence ascending like musical arpeggio
- Each jump must be timed progressively
- Beautiful rising movement
- **Priority:** Low

**12. Drop Dash**
- Jump from height, land on downbeat = massive dash
- Combines vertical and horizontal momentum
- Satisfying combo move
- **Priority:** Medium

**13. Fermata Freeze**
- Briefly freeze in mid-air on specific note
- Allows re-timing of landing
- Accessibility feature + style
- **Priority:** Medium

**14. Trill Flutter**
- Rapid alternating movement (left-right-left-right)
- Helicopter-like float
- Fun experimental movement
- **Priority:** Low

**15. Canon Roll**
- Roll that echoes delayed (clone appears doing same move)
- Visual musical callback
- Stylish dodge move
- **Priority:** Low

## Cross-System Integration

**Traversal + Combat:**
- Movement techniques usable in combat
- Combat abilities enhance exploration

**Crafting + Progression:**
- Crafted items unlock new abilities
- Progression unlocks crafting recipes

**Mini-Games + Relationships:**
- Play mini-games with NPCs for bonding
- Win competitions for reputation

**Everything + Rhythm:**
- All systems require rhythm mastery
- Musical skill improves all gameplay

---

## Unique Selling Points

1. **Every action is musical** - Even fishing and cooking
2. **World responds to rhythm** - Environment is instrument
3. **Crafting creates unique identity** - No two players have same equipment
4. **Mini-games aren't filler** - All teach rhythm concepts
5. **Depth without complexity** - Easy to start, hard to master

**Innovation:** The first game where crafting, cooking, and traversal all use rhythm mechanics meaningfully, not just cosmetically.
