## Naming Philosophy

**Guidelines:**
1. **Musical Connection** - Every name relates to music
2. **Memorable** - Easy to remember and pronounce
3. **Thematic** - Fits world and character
4. **Cultural Respect** - Real musical terms used correctly
5. **Variety** - Mix of familiar and invented names
6. **Genre Reflection** - Names reflect musical styles
7. **Avoid Clichés** - Fresh takes on musical terms
8. **Emotional Resonance** - Names evoke feelings
9. **Gameplay Clarity** - Item names describe function
10. **Worldbuilding** - Names tell story

**Process:**
1. Start with musical term/concept
2. Add fantasy/RPG twist
3. Check for trademark conflicts
4. Test pronunciation
5. Verify cultural sensitivity
6. Get feedback from musicians
7. Iterate and refine
