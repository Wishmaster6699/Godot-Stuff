## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Major Risks

**Risk 1: Scope Too Large**
- Mitigation: Phased releases (V1.1, 1.5, 2.0)
- Focus on core loop first
- Procedural generation for content padding

**Risk 2: Rhythm Alienates Non-Musicians**
- Mitigation: Generous timing windows
- Accessibility options
- Visual aids
- Tutorial excellence

**Risk 3: Monster Variety Too Demanding**
- Mitigation: Enemy variants reuse code
- Procedural generation
- Modular design system

**Risk 4: Budget/Team Size**
- Mitigation: Start small (V1.1 scope)
- Early Access funding
- Kickstarter potential
- MCP GDAI for efficiency

**Risk 5: Market Saturation**
- Mitigation: Unique positioning
- No direct competitor
- Multiple genre appeal
- Educational angle

**Risk 6: Technical Complexity**
- Mitigation: Godot expertise
- Proven systems (S01-S26)
- Modular architecture
- Extensive testing
