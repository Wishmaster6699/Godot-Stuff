## PRIMORDIAL LEGENDS (10)

### 1. **OMNIPHONIA** - The First Sound
- **Type:** Primordial/Sound
- **Size:** Universal (appears as 100ft being)
- **Lore:** The first vibration that created the universe. All music stems from its initial pulse. Said to exist before time, when silence was all that was.
- **Appearance:** Ever-shifting form of pure musical energy, combining all instruments, all voices, all rhythms into one transcendent being
- **Signature Move:** Genesis Chord - recreates the universe's first sound
- **Base Stats:** 680 total
- **Ability:** Omnisound - All sound-based moves deal 1.5x damage
- **How to Obtain:** Complete all 50 evolution chains, master all music styles, conduct perfect symphony

### 2. **SILENTIUS** - The Eternal Void
