## EVOLUTION METHODS ENCYCLOPEDIA (30+ Methods)

### Basic Methods
1. **Level-Based** - Reach specific level
2. **Happiness** - Max friendship/bond
3. **Item-Triggered** - Use evolution stone
4. **Trade** - Exchange with NPC

### Location-Based Methods
5. **Waterfall Evolution** - Level up near waterfall
6. **Volcano Evolution** - Evolve in hot areas
7. **Forest Evolution** - Deep in musical forest
8. **Cave Evolution** - Underground areas
9. **Sky Evolution** - High altitude locations
10. **Ocean Evolution** - Deep underwater

### Time-Based Methods
11. **Dawn Evolution** (5-7 AM)
12. **Day Evolution** (7 AM - 7 PM)
13. **Dusk Evolution** (7-9 PM)
14. **Night Evolution** (9 PM - 5 AM)
15. **Midnight Evolution** (exactly 12 AM)
16. **Seasonal Evolution** (Spring/Summer/Fall/Winter)

### Stat-Based Methods
17. **High Attack** - Attack > Defense at level-up
18. **High Defense** - Defense > Attack at level-up
19. **Balanced Stats** - All stats within 10% of each other
20. **High Rhythm** - Rhythm mastery A-rank or higher

### Battle-Based Methods
21. **Victory Evolution** - Win 100 battles
22. **Perfect Evolution** - Win with no damage
23. **Type Evolution** - Defeat 50 of specific enemy type
24. **Combo Evolution** - Achieve 100+ combo

### Item-Based Methods
25. **Held Item Evolution** - Hold specific item while leveling
26. **Equipment Evolution** - Wear specific gear
27. **Consumable Evolution** - Use special item

### Unique Methods
28. **Party Evolution** - Specific monsters in party
29. **Move Evolution** - Learn specific ability
30. **Ritual Evolution** - Perform specific action sequence
31. **Music Evolution** - Expose to specific genre
32. **Weather Evolution** - Evolve during rain/storm/sun
33. **Random Evolution** - Small % chance of rare path

## System Interactions

**Evolution → Combat:**
- Evolved forms learn new abilities
- Stats increase significantly
- New combat strategies unlock

**Evolution → Progression:**
- Evolution grants bonus XP
- Unlocks new skill tree branches
- Achievement rewards

**Evolution → Collection:**
- Completing evolution chains grants titles
- Rare evolutions tracked in Pokedex
- Shiny hunting encouraged

**Evolution → Economy:**
- Evolution items trade at high value
- Rare evolution paths = valuable monsters
- Breeding market for perfect evolution conditions

## Design Philosophy

✅ **Every evolution should feel meaningful**
