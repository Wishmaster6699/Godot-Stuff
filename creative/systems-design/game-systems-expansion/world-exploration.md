# World & Exploration
## 100+ Locations, Biomes, and Secrets

**Philosophy:** Every location should have personality and purpose
**Design Principle:** Exploration rewards curiosity and thorough investigation

---

## 1. MAJOR BIOMES (10 Locations)

**1. Harmony Haven** (Starting Town)
- Peaceful musical village where everyone lives in rhythm
- Tutorial area teaching basic mechanics
- Home base with shops, inn, storage
- *Safe hub with all amenities*

**2. Melodic Forest**
- Trees that hum, leaves that rustle musically
- Beginner-friendly monster spawns
- Hidden groves with rare herbs
- Wind chimes guide players to secrets
- *Verdant starter zone*

**3. Crescendo Peaks** (Mountain Range)
- Echo caves that amplify sound
- Avalanche rhythm puzzles
- Yeti Yodelers roam summit
- Thin air affects stamina (high altitude mechanic)
- *Vertical challenge zone*

**4. Dissonance Desert**
- Sandstorms that disrupt rhythm
- Mirages hide secrets
- Ancient ruins with musical puzzles
- Scorching heat drains stamina faster
- *Harsh survival biome*

**5. Legato Lagoon** (Ocean Zone)
- Underwater singing caves
- Coral chorus reefs
- Shipwrecks with treasure
- Requires swimming gear to explore fully
- *Aquatic exploration*

**6. Fortissimo Volcano**
- Lava flows pulse with beat
- Fire-type monster spawns
- Ancient forge for legendary crafting
- Heat requires fire-resistant gear
- *Dangerous endgame zone*

**7. Pianissimo Tundra**
- Quiet snowscapes, footsteps echo
- Aurora creates visual rhythm
- Ice caves with frozen treasures
- Cold slows movement without gear
- *Serene frozen wasteland*

**8. Staccato Steppes** (Plains)
- Tall grass rustles with rhythmic creatures
- Wide open spaces for mounted travel
- Nomadic tribes with unique music
- Weather changes frequently
- *Open exploration zone*

**9. Rhythm Ruins** (Ancient City)
- Crumbling civilization of musical masters
- Complex puzzles using old technology
- Ghost NPCs tell tragic history
- Legendary weapons hidden here
- *Lore-rich dungeon zone*

**10. Void Symphony** (Endgame Zone)
- Space/void themed area beyond reality
- Silence is deafening
- Hardest enemies and best loot
- Requires endgame power to survive
- *Ultimate challenge area*
