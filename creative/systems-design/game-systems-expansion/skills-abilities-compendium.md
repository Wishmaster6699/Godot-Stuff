## System Interactions

**Abilities → Progression:**
- Abilities level up with use
- Mastery unlocks enhanced versions
- Skill trees modify ability effects

**Abilities → Equipment:**
- Weapons determine available abilities
- Armor modifies cooldowns
- Accessories add passive effects

**Abilities → Rhythm:**
- Perfect timing enhances ability damage
- Rhythm gauge fills from combat
- Ultimate abilities require full gauge

**Abilities → Monsters:**
- Monsters learn new abilities when evolving
- Ability pool based on monster type
- Rare abilities from rare monsters

## Design Philosophy

✅ **Every ability should enable a playstyle**

✅ **Cooldowns prevent spam, encourage rotation**
✅ **Stamina creates resource management**
✅ **Ultimate abilities feel epic and impactful**
✅ **Passive abilities allow customization**
