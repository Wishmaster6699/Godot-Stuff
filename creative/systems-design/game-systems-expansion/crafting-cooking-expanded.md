## Recipe Discovery Methods

**1. Purchase from NPCs**
- Shops sell common recipes
- Rare merchants have unique recipes

**2. Find in World**
- Chests contain recipe scrolls
- Books in libraries teach recipes

**3. Experimentation**
- Try random ingredient combos
- Failed attempts still recorded

**4. Quest Rewards**
- Complete quests for recipes
- Some NPCs teach when befriended

**5. Monster Drops**
- Rare monsters drop recipe scrolls
- Boss drops = legendary recipes

**6. Crafting Milestones**
- Craft 100 items → unlock advanced recipes
- Mastery rewards new options

**7. Seasonal Events**
- Holiday recipes available during events
- Limited-time cooking

**8. Achievement Unlocks**
- Complete collections → recipe rewards
- Perfect runs unlock secrets

## Quality Tier System

**Normal (60-79% rhythm accuracy)**
- Standard item/meal
- Base effects
- Common result

**Fine (80-89% accuracy)**
- +20% stats/duration
- Slightly better
- Good result

**Superior (90-95% accuracy)**
- +50% stats/duration
- Great result
- Uncommon

**Masterwork (96-99% accuracy)**
- +100% stats/duration
- Rare quality
- Impressive

**Legendary (100% perfect)**
- +200% stats/duration
- Unique effects added
- Ultimate quality

**Failed (<60% accuracy)**
- Degraded item (still usable, -50% effectiveness)
- Encourages practice
- Materials not wasted
